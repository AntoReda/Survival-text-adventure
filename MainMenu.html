Endings Unlocked:

A Day In The Life by Antonio Reda

Welcome to this text based survival game. This game will test your ability to survive and excel as a University student. There are 6 total endings.

Each ending has an image associated with it. Once you find an image, it will appear at the top of this page.

Once you find 5 of the endings, a Quiz button will appear in order to assess your learning.
The reason why you need to complete 5 and not 6 is because one ending is hidden.
Try your best to find it!
